# Business Info

## Organization Overview

This is a **white-label AI ad creation tool** — a SaaS product that helps brands create effective social media ads (Facebook/Instagram). Users bring their own brand, and the tool handles everything: understanding the brand identity, researching competitors, and generating ad concepts with AI-written copy and AI-generated visuals.

The tool is designed so that **other people can use it with their own brands** — it's not just for one brand. Any user should be able to plug in a website URL and Instagram handle and get a complete brand profile, product catalog, and visual asset library built automatically.

## Product / Focus Areas

- **Brand Intelligence**: Automated brand scraping — crawl a website, extract products, download visuals, analyze brand identity with AI
- **Competitor Research**: Find and analyze competitor ads via Meta Ad Library keyword search
- **Ad Knowledge Base**: Expert tactics extracted from YouTube videos on ad creation (foundational, one-time data)
- **Ad Concept Generation**: AI-generated ad copy (headline, body, CTA) and visuals powered by Claude + Kie.ai
- **Beginner Tips**: Auto-generated educational content for users new to advertising

## Key Context

- Early-stage product, actively being built feature-by-feature
- Solo developer (Oleg) building the full stack
- Currently functional but being refined for polish and usability — the user cares deeply about the product looking professional, not "half-baked"
- The workflow is being broken into smaller, clearer steps rather than one monolithic pipeline
